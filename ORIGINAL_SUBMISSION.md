## Idea
Problem Statement:
Indian farmers often choose crops based on past experience, seasonal habits, or local advice without access to complete data such as soil condition, weather suitability, irrigation availability, mandi prices, seed quality, fertilizer needs, and market demand. This information gap can lead to low profits, crop oversupply, improper fertilizer usage, and unstable farmer income. There is a need for a smart digital system that helps farmers make crop decisions based on both agricultural suitability and economic profitability.

Proposed Solution:
AgriSarthi AI is an AI-powered mobile application that recommends the most suitable and profitable crops for farmers based on location, soil type, season, irrigation availability, mandi price trends, seed availability, fertilizer requirements, and market demand. The app provides top crop recommendations with profit score, market demand score, and risk score, along with simple explanations in regional languages. It also suggests suitable fertilizers, seeds, nearby mandi price comparisons, and future alerts for weather or price risks.

Real-Life Problem It Solves:
A farmer may grow a crop only because it was grown successfully in the previous season, but later face losses due to low mandi price, unsuitable soil, high fertilizer cost, or oversupply in the market. AgriSarthi AI solves this by guiding the farmer before cultivation, helping them choose a crop that is suitable for their land and more profitable in the current market. This improves farmer income, reduces unplanned crop production, supports better fertilizer usage, and helps balance agricultural supply with national demand.
